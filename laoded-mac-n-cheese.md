# Loaded Mac N Cheese
### Ingredients
- 2 ib chicken
- 2 box elbow pasta
- olive oil
- 1 onion
- 4 cloves garlic
- 6tbsp flour
- 4 cup milk
- 1 tbsp dried mustard
- 4 cups mozzarellla cheese
- 2 cups cheddar cheese
- 1/2 cup parmesean cheese

### Instructions
- cook pasta
- cook chicken, onions, garlic in skillet. season with salt & pepper. add flour, cook for another minute or so
- add milk, then mustard. add mozzarella and cheddar cheese. cook until cheese is fully melted
