# Rice In A Pot
### Ingredients
- rice
- water
- pot

### Instructions
1. add rice to pot. rinse several times. note that dry rice expands to about twice its original size
2. add water such that the water level is 1" above the rice level
3. add lid, bring to a boil, then simmer (should be consistent bubbles when simmering)
4. simmer for 10 minutes WITH THE LID ON. make sure that the rice is cooked all the way through by biting a grain of rice in half, and making sure that it is cooked all the way through.
5. remove the lid, keep the heat the same, and cook for another 2 minutes
6. remove the rice from the heat, mix & eat
