# Japanese Golden Curry
### Ingredients
- 1.25ib chicken
- 1 small russet potato
- 1 carrot (about the same amount of carrot as potato)
- 1 onion
- 1 box s&b golden curry mix
- 1.25 cups dry rice

### Instructions
1. cook rice as desired
2. sear chicken, diced onion, sliced carrot, & diced potato in large skillet with vegetable oil for ~5min
3. add 2 1/4 cups water, place lid on skillet & bring to boil
4. reduce heat & simmer for 15 mins WITH LID ON, or until potatoes are cooked through
5. break the curry mix bars into small chunks, add & mix until fully incorporated. it may take 2-3 minutes of stirring for the curry paste to fully dissolve & thicken.
6. serve curry over a bed of rice
