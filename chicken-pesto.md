# Chicken Pesto
### Ingredients
- 2.5 ib chicken
- 1.5 ib pasta (rotini is the classic)
- 2c cream
- 2 c mozzarella
- 3/4 cup pesto

### Instructions
1. make the pasta according to instructions on the box. strain and place back in the pot
2. cook the chicken in a large skillet. season with italian seasoning, salt & pepper
3. once the chicken is almost cooked, add all the cream, mozzarella, and pesto. stir until it becomes a thick sauce. simmer for 1/2 minutes, then add to the pot of noodles. mix until thoroughly incorporated
