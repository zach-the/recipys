# Cajun Shrimp Pasta
### Materials
- blender/food processor
- large bowl
- 12" deep skillet

### Ingredients
- 2ib shrimp
- 4tsp slap ya mama
- 3/4 cup olive oil
- 1 large onion, coarsely chopped
- 2 red bell pepper, coarsely chopped
- 4 garlic cloves
- 24oz pasta (linguine)
- 2c heavy cream
- 6oz parmesan cheese
- 1c finely chopped parsley
- 1c finely chopped green onion

### Instructions
- season shrimp in a bowl with cajun, 4tbsp oil, and salt
- cook shrimp in batches in a large skillet, browning each side of the shrimp (60s/side)
- pulse onion, bell pepper, and garlic, until a smooth puree is formed
- cook pasta in salt water, save 1 cup of pasta water
- add oil to hot skillet, cook onion blend. add water as needed to keep the mixture from burning, until the water is evaporated and mixture becomes drier and thicker (should take 7-10 mins)
- add cream, bring to a boil, then reduce heat. melt parmesan into sauce. add shrimp, pasta, parsley, green onion, and ~3/4c pasta water, until sauce is right consistency
