# Fajita Stir Fry
### Ingredients
- 2c dry rice
- 2ib chicken 
- 3 bell peppers
- 1 onion
- 2 packets siete family foods chicken fajita seasoning
- 1 can black beans
- 1 can sweet corn
- 2 20oz cans las palmas green enchilada sauce
- mexican cheese
- sour cream
- guacamole
- salsa

### Instructions
1. cook rice using desired method. once rice has cooked, add desired amount of enchilada sauce and mix until thoroughly incorporated
2. heat beans & corn in a bowl together
3. add chicken, bell peppers, and onions to a hot skillet with olive oil. add fajita seasoning
4. serve the stir fry over the rice bed with the beans & corn. top as desired with cheese, sour cream, guacamole, and salsa
